Meet the Bhookies

Welcome to Bhookie – Taste the Fusion!

We’re not just a restaurant; we’re a celebration of bold flavors, street-style creativity, and global fusion on your plate.

Born out of a love for food that brings people together, Bhookie brings the punch of Indian street food and the flair of international fast bites right to the heart of Liverpool. From spicy Peri Peri Chicken Bites and crisps fries to our signature Mumbai Wraps and the ever-iconic Vada Pav, every dish is crafted to deliver comfort with a kick.

Our inspiration comes from the vibrant lanes of Mumbai and the global streets of London, Bangkok, and New York—places where cultures collide and food tells stories. Fusion isn’t a trend for us; it’s a tradition.

Throughout history, food has evolved through travel, trade, and storytelling—and Bhookie continues that journey by bringing together bold Indian spices with global fast-food favorites in a way that’s new, exciting, and deliciously familiar.
